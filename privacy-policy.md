# Privacy Policy — ADS GD Pin Automation

**Last updated:** July 2026

This is a personal automation tool used to create and manage pins on a single, personally-owned Pinterest account. It is not a public-facing application and does not collect, store, or process data from any third-party users.

## What this tool does
- Connects to one Pinterest account via Pinterest's official OAuth process
- Creates pins containing product names, images, and affiliate links (Amazon Associates)
- Does not collect personal data from Pinterest users, visitors, or any third party

## Data handling
- Pinterest OAuth tokens are stored locally and securely on the developer's own device
- No data is sold, shared, or transferred to any third party
- No analytics or tracking is performed on Pinterest users interacting with the pins
